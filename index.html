About Me

I am an avid equestrian and am building an app to resolve problems for people who show. will then use the profits to rescue horses from slaughter. I started my career creating Websites and then fell into enterprise software sales. After something like 25 years doing that, I have done well and made enough money to start this business. Now I am going use this knowledge to create an app which solves many problems inherent in showing. If it's successful, then the profits from it will be used to rescue horses from slaughter.
